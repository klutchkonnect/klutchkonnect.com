Born in Lagos. Built under pressure. Klutch Konnect is a closed-loop ecosystem connecting agriculture, logistics, youth sports, and elite networking — all from one lightning bolt.

FROM ZERO TO ECOSYSTEM

Adetunji Odusami bootstrapped ₦2.5M to build something Lagos had never seen — not just a farm, not just a logistics company, not just a sports platform, but all four working together as one.

Starting from Jakande Estate, Isolo, every naira went into proving the concept. Roasted fish was the first product. Real customers. Real revenue. Real proof.

Today, Klutch Konnect Ltd is CAC registered, FIRS verified, SCUML certified, and generating revenue across multiple arms. This is just the beginning.
